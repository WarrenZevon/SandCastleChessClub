Welcome to Sand Castle Chess Club

Calling all young chess players in grades K-4! Join our fun and friendly chess club where kids learn, play, and make new friends. No experience needed - we'll teach you all about the exciting world of chess!

Club Meetings

Come join us for chess fun every week!

  • When: Every Sunday, 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM
  • Where: 860 Industrial Way, San Luis Obispo, CA
  • Who: Students in Kindergarten through 4th grade
  • What to bring: Just your smile! We provide all chess equipment

Meet Coach Dave

With 20 years of experience teaching chess to students, Coach Dave brings passion and expertise to every lesson. His patient teaching style and deep understanding of the game help young players develop both their chess skills and strategic thinking abilities.
